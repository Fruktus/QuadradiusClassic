TIMESTAMPS



Shawn Tanner (AfroNinja) of JayIsGames.com gave us a lengthy review. He covered many aspects of QR, from art, to strategy, to Memberships. Even has some critiques. A downright honest reviewer!

JIG (JayIsGames) doesn’t just review any flash game that comes across their desktops. They seriously hold a higher standard that is obvious in the first few seconds of scrolling through their site. There is a plethora of decent sized screen grabs of all of their reviewed games, so you can easily find something of interest. Each review contains paragraphs dedicated to both the games description along with their honest review and feedback. Having so many professional, enthusiastic reviewers on this site allows them to have about 1 or 2 very in-depth, worthwhile reviews each day! Great navigation, colorful, an overall eye-pleasing site for finding great games on the internet.



Tom Fulp of Newgrounds.com wrote a blurb about QR on his homepage, forum AND newsletter! It is this very review that put QR on the map.

Newgrounds has been around for over a decade, and is the original and largest Flash portal on the internet, from animations and audio to casual games and full fledge console games. As the creators of Alien Hominid and Castle Crashers, this team is continually evolving its website and content to reflect a large scale interaction of users. From Rankings, Whistle-blowing, voting up great stuff and voting down the garbage, the users are heavily involved in shaping the portal to be a great resource for anything flash based. No doubt you have seen something from Newgrounds in the past, for they get half a million people visiting their site daily!



Psycho Goldfish of WebGameMagazine.com wrote an awesome article right on their homepage, placing us in the first position of "Great Games", and giving us a 9.5 out of 10 on a pretty strict scale!

WebGameMagazine reviews a handful of games every week, fulfilling your continual drive for new and fun internet time wasters. They tag all the games and group them into categories, for easy pickins. Also, you will notice a very visual ranking system, along with grouping top games into different popular categories with colorful icons. Makes it easy to find little gems.
